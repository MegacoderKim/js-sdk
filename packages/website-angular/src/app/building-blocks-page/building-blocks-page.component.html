Building blocks

Build your use case with one or more of these primitives

Users

When you start tracking users, you get their activity, location, and health. You can get them in realtime or historically with Placeline.

Activity

Know what the user is doing, e.g. walk, run, drive and stop.

Location

Know where the user is. Get the time-aware polyline for each activity segment.

Health

Keep tabs on critical health parameters of the device.

ACTIONS

When you start tracking actions, you get its ETA, mileage and status. In addition, you get activity, location and health of the user to whom the action is assigned.

ETA

Get smart ETAs that factor vehicle type, user profile and the time it takes to get in and out of addresses. ETA for each action becomes available on assignment.

Mileage

Get accurate distance and time traveled for each action. Use it to charge customers, reimburse expenses or get trip summary.

Status

Get real-time status when unexpected things happen along the way, e.g. delay, completed far away, etc.

HOW YOU CONSUME

You can consume the metadata for users and actions as data, visuals and events.

Data

Get data on your backend server through REST APIs or in your mobile app through SDK methods.

VISUALS

Get beautiful maps with individual and aggregate views that integrates with your product experience and dashboards.

EVENTS

Get events delivered to your server through webhooks, or directly to your Slack channel.
